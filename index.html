Welcome!

I'm Paola, a Master's student in Theoretical and Mathematical Physics at LMU and TUM in Munich, currently completing my Master's thesis in the Quantum Field Theory group at the Max Planck Institute for Physics, supervised by Prof. Dr. Johannes Henn and Dr. Fabian Schmidt.

In a nutshell:

  • I did my Bachelor's in Physics at the University of Trento, then moved to Munich for the TMP Master's program. This autumn, I will continue with a PhD at the Max Planck Institute for Physics, supervised by Prof. Dr. Johannes Henn and co-supervised by Prof. Lorenzo Tancredi, focusing on surfaceology. Have a look at my CV for a summary.
  • Broadly, I am interested in scattering amplitudes and Feynman integrals. On one side, cosmology and the EFT of Large-Scale Structure, where I work on higher-loop corrections — my Master's thesis is on the two-loop matter power spectrum. On the other, more formal aspects of scattering amplitudes, in particular positive geometries and surfaceology, which I'm looking forward to exploring further during my PhD. See my research page for details!
  • I also keep a running collection of notes and resources I've found useful along the way.
