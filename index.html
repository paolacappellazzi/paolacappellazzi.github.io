About

I am a Master's student in Theoretical and Mathematical Physics at LMU and TUM in Munich, currently completing my Master's thesis in the Quantum Field Theory group at the Max Planck Institute for Physics, supervised by Prof. Dr. Johannes Henn and Dr. Fabian Schmidt.

  • 2020 – 2023

    PhD, Max Planck Institute for Physics, supervised by Prof. Dr. Johannes Henn, co-supervised by Prof. Lorenzo Tancredi, focusing on surfaceology

Research interests

Broadly, I am interested in scattering amplitudes and Feynman integrals. On one side, cosmology and the Effective Field Theory of Large-Scale Structure (EFTofLSS), in particular computing higher-loop corrections — in my Master's thesis I worked on the two-loop matter power spectrum. On the other, more formal aspects of scattering amplitudes, especially positive geometries and surfaceology, which I am looking forward to exploring further during my PhD.
